Gilgamesh’s Journey

Follow the major stages of Gilgamesh’s physical and emotional journey.

← Back to The Epic of Gilgamesh
🏛️
Tablets I–II

Uruk

Gilgamesh rules harshly until Enkidu arrives, confronts him, and becomes his closest companion.

🛡️
Tablet III

Preparing to Leave

The elders advise the heroes, and Ninsun asks Shamash to protect them during the expedition.

🏜️
Tablet IV

The Wilderness

Gilgamesh and Enkidu travel across great distances while dreams reveal their fear of the danger ahead.

🌲
Tablet V

The Cedar Forest

The heroes confront Humbaba, kill the forest’s guardian, and cut down the sacred cedars.

🐂
Tablet VI

Return to Uruk

Gilgamesh rejects Ishtar, and the two heroes defeat the Bull of Heaven.

🕯️
Tablets VII–VIII

Enkidu’s Death

Enkidu dies after the gods judge the heroes, leaving Gilgamesh overwhelmed by grief and fear.

⛰️
Tablet IX

Mount Mashu

Gilgamesh abandons Uruk and crosses the dark path of the sun while searching for immortality.

🍷
Tablet X

Siduri and Ur-shanabi

Siduri directs Gilgamesh toward the boatman who can carry him across the Waters of Death.

🌊
Tablet X

The Waters of Death

Gilgamesh crosses the deadly waters and finally reaches Uta-napishti.

🌿
Tablet XI

Immortality Denied

Gilgamesh learns the flood story, fails the test of sleep, and loses the plant that restores youth.

🧱
End of Tablet XI

Return to Uruk

Gilgamesh returns home and recognizes the lasting value of human work, community, and memory.
